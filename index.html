About Me

I am a seasoned data professional bridging the gap between complex information and actionable insights. My background in psychology and sociology informs my approach to data analysis and application development, enabling me to consider the human element in every project.

With 12 years of experience as a Senior Data Analyst at Oracle Cerner, I'm adept at designing, implementing, validating, and presenting data-driven solutions that meet diverse organizational needs. My technical skills encompass a wide range of tools and technologies, including SQL, Python, Generative AI, and cloud computing platforms.

My journey into application development is driven by a passion for building the tools that bring data to life. I am excited to leverage my deep understanding of data structures, ETL processes, and user needs to create efficient, intuitive, and impactful applications.
